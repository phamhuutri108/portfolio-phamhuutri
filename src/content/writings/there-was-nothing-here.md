<div class="content-vi" lang="vi">
<p>there was nothing here.</p>
            <p>no being, no emptiness.</p>
            <p>no space, no time.</p>
            <p>there was no light to be seen for</p>
            <p>no darkness to be found.</p>
            <p>no good and no evil.</p>
            <p>no life, no death.</p>
            <p>no me, no you, no one.</p>
            <p>there was no reason to exist, and</p>
            <p>there was no non-existent one.</p>
            <p>no.</p>
</div>
